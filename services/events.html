Aerial Event Photography

Cinematic 4K Footage

Smooth, stabilized clips designed for highlight reels and social media.

  • Slow cinematic passes

  • Optional vertical clips for social

Safety & Flight Planning

Every event flight includes mission planning, risk assessment, and airspace review.

  • FAA compliance / Part 107 operations
  • Airspace authorization (LAANC) when needed
  • Coordination with event staff for safe zones

What You’ll Receive

  • Edited high-resolution images
  • Optional 4K video clips (or a short highlight reel)
  • Organized delivery in labeled folders
  • Web-ready versions for posting and sharing

Note: Some events may require additional coordination depending on location, crowd density, and airspace. We’ll confirm feasibility and safety requirements during scheduling.
