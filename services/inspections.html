Roof Inspections

Detailed imagery of roofing systems without requiring ladder access or putting personnel at risk.

  • Close-up inspection of shingles, tiles, and membranes
  • Identify damage, wear, and potential issues
  • Document conditions for insurance claims
  • Pre-sale or post-storm assessments

Tower & Structure Inspections

Visual inspection of cell towers, water tanks, bridges, and other tall or difficult-to-access structures.

  • Safer alternative to climbing
  • Detailed imagery of welds, connections, and surfaces
  • Identify corrosion, cracks, or damage
  • Faster than traditional inspection methods

Building & Facility Inspections

Comprehensive visual surveys of commercial buildings, warehouses, and industrial facilities.

  • Exterior wall and facade inspection
  • Gutter, downspout, and drainage assessment
  • HVAC and rooftop equipment documentation
  • Site condition reports for stakeholders

What You'll Receive

  • High-resolution images of inspected areas
  • Optional video walkthroughs showing key features
  • Organized delivery with labeled sections
  • Annotations and notes available upon request
  • Documentation suitable for reports and insurance claims

Note: Drone inspections provide excellent visual documentation but are not a substitute for licensed engineering assessments when required. We can work with your inspectors and engineers to provide supporting imagery and data.
